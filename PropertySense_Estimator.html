How It Works

Our model is trained on 21,613 real property transactions using a Random Forest ensemble algorithm.

📊

Trained on Real Data

The model learned patterns from over 21,000 actual residential sales. It understands how quality grade, square footage, and location interact to determine value.

🌲

Random Forest Algorithm

We use an ensemble of 20 decision trees that vote on the final price. This reduces overfitting and handles non-linear relationships better than simple regression.

The model explains 86.7% of price variance. The average prediction error (MAE) is $66,461 — substantially better than a simple linear model at $106,185 MAE.
